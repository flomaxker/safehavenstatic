Integration Flashcards

Moving to a new country, especially the Netherlands, comes with unique questions and challenges for expat families. These flashcards cover common issues Third Culture Kids (TCKs) and their parents might face when integrating in Groningen. Click on a card to reveal a helpful tip or starting point.

School placement difficulties?

School placement difficulties?

Contact Gemeente Groningen (Youth & Education) or school admissions for placement support.

Dutch school system?

Dutch school system?

Understand the system: CITO tests, school types, parent involvement. See expat guide to Dutch education .

Child's language barrier?

Child's language barrier?

Explore Inburgeren in Groningen for language options, buddies, or bilingual resources.

Feeling culturally isolated?

Feeling culturally isolated?

Join expat groups via IWCN , pursue hobbies, connect locally.

Finding a good bike?

Finding a good bike?

Check local 'Fietsenwinkel', Marktplaats.nl , or expat swap groups.

Registration confusion?

Registration confusion?

Register at Gemeente Groningen (Moving) for BSN.

Healthcare navigation?

Healthcare navigation?

Register with a 'huisarts' (GP) after BSN. Understand 'eigen risico'. ACCESS NL info .

Child's homesickness?

Child's homesickness?

Validate feelings, keep routines, connect home, make new Groningen memories.

Identity confusion surfacing?

Identity confusion surfacing?

Explore TCK identity positively. Find TCK resources/support groups (e.g., via IWCN).

Difficulty making friends?

Difficulty making friends?

Encourage local sports, clubs, or after-school activities. Arrange playdates.

Relocation fatigue kicking in?

Relocation fatigue kicking in?

Prioritize rest & self-care. Acknowledge the toll of moving. Lower expectations.

Emotional outburst triggers?

Emotional outburst triggers?

Help child name feelings & use calming breaths. Validate emotion, address root cause.

Attachment issues arising?

Attachment issues arising?

Provide consistent presence & routines. Express love. Seek professional help if needed.

Behavioral changes at school?

Behavioral changes at school?

Communicate with child & teacher ('juf'/'meester'). Find strategies together.

Difficulty finding a therapist?

Difficulty finding a therapist?

Consult 'huisarts' for GGZ referral. Search online (Expatica Directory) .

Anxiety about leaving again?

Anxiety about leaving again?

Discuss future plans (age-appropriate). Focus on present, build resilience for transitions.

Understanding Dutch directness?

Understanding Dutch directness?

Explain directness isn't rude. Focus on intent, role-play responses, teach clear communication.

Social events feeling awkward?

Social events feeling awkward?

Prepare for events. Set small goals. Focus on activities & celebrate effort.

Limited extracurricular activities?

Limited extracurricular activities?

Explore Gemeente Groningen (Culture, Sport & Leisure) , community centers, libraries.

Safety concerns downtown?

Safety concerns downtown?

Teach local safety (esp. bike rules!). Walk routes, set clear rules & meeting points.

Homesickness, Dutch Food?

Homesickness, Dutch Food?

Mix familiar home foods & Dutch treats. Connect food to cultural exploration.

Sadness, grey weather?

Sadness, grey weather?

Embrace 'gezelligheid'. Get outside (gear up!). Plan fun. Consider light/Vit D.
