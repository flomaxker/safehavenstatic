🙋🏼‍♂️About Me

Hallo! Ik ben Florian.

I’m the heart behind Safe Haven Dutch Coaching — a personalized language and integration service designed especially for expat and Third Culture Kid (TCK) children living in Groningen.

After years of teaching Dutch to adults, I’ve refocused my mission on the ones who often need it most: international children adjusting to a new life, language, and school system. My goal is simple — to help your child not only learn Dutch, but truly feel at home here.

💬A Language Journey That’s Playful, Purposeful, and Personal

Learning Dutch shouldn't feel like a chore — especially not for kids. My sessions are built around playful, interactive, real-world learning, tailored to each child’s age, language level, and personality.

Think: baking Dutch cookies while learning verbs, role-playing playground conversations, or going on cultural outings where the language comes to life. I don’t just teach vocabulary — I coach children in confidence, communication, and connection.

🌱More Than Language: Creating a Sense of Home

Grounding myself in the Netherlands taught me that home isn’t just a place — it’s a feeling of safety, identity, and belonging.

That’s what I want to give your child. Every session is designed not only to teach Dutch, but to build resilience, emotional safety, and social ease. Whether your child is new to the country, starting Dutch school, or somewhere in between, we provide the soft landing they need to adapt and grow.

🧭A Holistic Approach for TCKs

Safe Haven Dutch Coaching blends NT2 language learning with emotional and social integration — all rooted in a gentle, child-centered coaching philosophy.

Inspired by a holistic view of well-being, I focus on nurturing each child’s inner confidence, language fluency, and sense of connection — in a way that respects all cultural backgrounds and honors the whole child.

Please note: Our coaching provides educational and emotional support and is not a substitute for therapy. We focus on language acquisition, cultural integration, and fostering well-being within an educational context. If deeper concerns arise, I always refer to licensed professionals.

🛡️Your Child’s Safety Comes First

At Safe Haven Dutch Coaching, your child’s safety and well-being are really important to me. I work with clear boundaries, keep parents in the loop, and do everything I can to create a space that feels safe and supportive. I have a current VOG (Dutch certificate of good conduct) and First Aid certification for children, so I’m prepared if anything unexpected happens.

I also take privacy seriously. Any info you share is stored safely and only used with your permission. It’s all about building trust — so you and your child can feel at ease from day one.

👪Who I Work With

My services are now fully focused on:

  • Expat children aged 4–16 attending Dutch or international schools
  • Third Culture Kids navigating school and social life in a new language
  • Families planning a longer stay in the Netherlands who want their children to truly thrive — not just “get by”
