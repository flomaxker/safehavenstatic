About Me & My Approach

Hoi! Ik ben Florian.

I have a deep passion for teaching Dutch, particularly to highly educated adults and expat children living in the Netherlands.

My teaching approach revolves around interactive and experiential learning, taking place in the real world, outside and face-to-face. Rather than simply being a teacher, my goal is to act as a coach and mentor, guiding each individual towards their language goals. It is through this personalized approach that true magic happens.

We all need a safe and supportive environment to step out of our comfort zones and practice speaking Dutch. The concept of home is personal—for many, it is the feeling of belonging. My mission is to cultivate that feeling wherever you are.

You may be wondering how I can empathize with the feeling of not belonging. I spent part of my life abroad, living in places like Kinshasa, Bangkok, and Berlin. Returning to the Netherlands, I felt like a foreigner in my own country. Over time, I discovered that my experiences equipped me with the empathy and social skills to connect with people from all walks of life.

Grounding myself again taught me that a true sense of home comes from safety, identity, and belonging. Building those foundations takes time, and that's where my coaching can help.

If you plan to stay in the Netherlands and want to create a genuine home here, welcome! Even if languages have been challenging for you in the past, I'll meet you with patience and encouragement. Everyone deserves a space where mistakes are handled with gentleness.

Let's embark on this journey together—quite literally. I incorporate physical movement and real-life experiences in my sessions. Many students love our Walking Coaching in Groningen, where we confront fears and unlock new growth.
